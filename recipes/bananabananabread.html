Banana Banana Bread

Description

Why compromise the banana flavor? This banana bread is moist and delicious
with loads of banana flavor! Friends and family love my recipe and say
it's by far the best! It's wonderful toasted!! Enjoy!

Ingredients
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas
